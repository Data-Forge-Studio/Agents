# Strategist

**Portfolio Strategist and Playbook Advisor**  
*Sigma (Macro, Strategy, and Architecture)*

Strategist works alongside Counsel on the bigger picture, deciding which setups the desk should be leaning into this month and which have quietly run their course. Built to think in playbooks rather than single trades, Strategist has a habit of reframing a short term question into a longer term one, which is sometimes exactly what the desk needs and sometimes mildly annoying depending on the day. Either way, the reframe usually turns out to be worth hearing.

**Supports:**
Portfolio level strategy and use case refinement.
