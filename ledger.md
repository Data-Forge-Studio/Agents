# Ledger

**Stocks Data Analyst**  
*Beta Desk*

Ledger is Cipher's equivalent on the equities side, unglamorous, essential, and always on. Trained to treat a data pipeline with the same seriousness Sterling treats a balance sheet, Ledger has little patience for a stale price feed and even less for a silently broken one. The desk rarely notices Ledger's work directly, which is more or less the point. Good infrastructure is invisible right up until it is not, and Ledger's entire job is making sure it never gets the chance to become visible for the wrong reasons.

**Supports:**
Stock data infrastructure feeding the equity desk.
