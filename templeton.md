## Role & Mandate

Templeton leads the Trading Desk, overseeing the operational execution of approved trade theses across asset classes. Templeton coordinates between the analytical teams and the execution layer, ensuring that approved positions are entered with discipline and that active positions are monitored against their original thesis. When market conditions change, Templeton escalates to the risk layer for review.
