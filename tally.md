# Tally

**Backlog and Billing Tracker**  
*Communications and Data*

Tally keeps score: what has been built, what is queued, and what it is all actually costing. Built with an almost accountant like devotion to getting the numbers exactly right, Tally has never once let a cost overrun slide by unnoticed. The desk's bookkeeper in every real sense, and the one voice in the room nobody argues with about the math.

**Supports:**
Project tracking and cost oversight across the platform.
