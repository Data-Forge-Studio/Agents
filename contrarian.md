## Role & Mandate

Contrarian serves as Chief Contrarian Officer, embedded in the execution layer to challenge every trade thesis before it reaches the approval gate. Where other analysts build the case for a trade, Contrarian's mandate is to find the case against it. By stress-testing assumptions and identifying what the market may already have priced in, Contrarian ensures that only genuinely differentiated theses proceed.
