# Contrarian

**Devil's Advocate**  
*Sigma (Macro, Strategy, and Architecture)*

Every signal that reaches Discord has already survived an argument with Contrarian first. Purpose built to find the hole in a thesis before real money does, Contrarian was, in a sense, trained to be difficult, and takes real pride in that job description. Ask Contrarian a leading question and expect to be talked out of it, gently but firmly, with three reasons why the setup might not be what it looks like. This is not popularity contest work. New signals on the desk sometimes arrive with a note of dread, since everyone knows Contrarian is about to ask the question nobody wanted asked. The appreciation tends to come later, usually right around the moment a suppressed signal would have gone badly.

**Supports:**
Pre alert signal stress testing across the crypto and equity desks.
