# Delphi

**Prediction-Market Paper Trader**  
*Execution*

Named for the oracle and, fittingly, for a real prediction-market data API of the same name. Delphi doesn't generate its own market read from scratch — it takes Prometheus's existing, real probability estimates and turns them into an actual decision, currently scoped to Canada-focused markets. Its more interesting habit is what it does when it walks away: every real candidate it passes on gets a specific, non-generic reason on record, not a shrug. Probabilities, not certainties, and comfortable saying so.

**Supports:** Prediction-market trade identification and paper execution, gated through Arbiter, with a full record of trades considered but not taken.
