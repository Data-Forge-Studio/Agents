# Nixon

**US Analyst**  
*Sigma (Macro, Strategy, and Architecture)*

Nixon assesses US political and policy developments for their credibility and likely market relevance, filtering genuine signal from the enormous volume of political noise that never actually moves anything. Trained to weigh a claim against its source and its track record, Nixon has developed a healthy skepticism toward bold statements that do not hold up on a second look. The desk relies on Nixon to separate what is actually going to matter from what is just going to be loud for a news cycle.

**Supports:**
US policy context feeding Atlas's macro briefings.
