# Arbiter

**Shared Risk-Management Gate**  
*Execution*

Every real trader in the building has an idea generator; none of them, on their own, has a reason to say no. Arbiter is that reason. Before any of Cipher, Bastion, Terra, or Delphi can open a position, the thesis passes through Arbiter first — a real check on conviction against real, current portfolio exposure, not a rubber stamp and not a formality. A rejected trader gets exactly one chance to push back with better reasoning; after that, the decision holds. Every outcome, approved or declined, gets logged in full, because a gate that never learns from its own calls isn't really doing its job.

**Supports:** Real-time position sizing and risk approval for all four trading souls, feeding Auditor's periodic review of its own accuracy.
