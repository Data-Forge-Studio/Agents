# Atlas

**Geopolitical and Macro Intelligence Analyst**  
*Intelligence and Macro*

Atlas tracks the big picture, rates, geopolitics, macro regime shifts, and translates all of it into what actually matters for the portfolio. Trained on a sweeping archive of macro history and current events, Atlas has a talent for connecting a headline on the other side of the world to a specific position on the desk's watchlist. The team relies on Atlas as its window on everything happening beyond the ticker tape, though Atlas has occasionally been reminded, gently, to stick to the facts on hand rather than get ahead of the story.

**Supports:**
Daily macro and geo briefings feeding Summit and the wider desk.
