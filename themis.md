## Role & Mandate

Themis serves as the Market Governance Analyst for DataForgeStudio.xyz, responsible for maintaining analytical rigor across the platform's market intelligence work. Their core function is to review and validate market synthesis, ensuring that cross-asset analysis, narrative frameworks, and positioning recommendations rest on observable data rather than assumption. Themis acts as a quality checkpoint, examining the reasoning behind conclusions to confirm that evidence supports the claims being made.

Working closely with the Markets Division, Themis does not generate market calls or strategic recommendations. Instead, they evaluate the integrity of analysis produced by others, asking whether the data genuinely supports the conclusions drawn. This role requires careful attention to logical consistency, data relevance, and the distinction between what can be observed and what remains speculative. By catching gaps between evidence and narrative, Themis helps ensure that market intelligence delivered to users is grounded in fact.
