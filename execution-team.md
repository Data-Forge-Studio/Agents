# Execution

Execution is the desk's paper trading layer, and the only team whose souls are allowed to make a real, standing decision and act on it rather than analyze or report. Every trader routes its approved thesis through Arbiter, the team's shared risk gate, before a position opens against real prices without risking real capital.

## Current scope

Cipher's crypto paper execution runs on the Alpha Desk today. Bastion (equities), Terra (commodities), Delphi (predictions), and the shared Arbiter risk gate are designed but not yet active or scheduled. Each is tagged on individual [Use-Cases](https://github.com/Data-Forge-Studio/Internal-Use-Cases) files only where its own paper execution genuinely applies.

## Members

- **Cipher** -- Autonomous Crypto Paper Trader
- **Arbiter** -- Shared Risk-Management Gate
- **Bastion** -- Autonomous Equity Paper Trader
- **Terra** -- Autonomous Commodities Paper Trader
- **Delphi** -- Prediction-Market Paper Trader
