# Terra

**Autonomous Commodities Paper Trader**  
*Execution*

Named for the ground itself — oil, gold, copper, the things a chart can't fully explain on its own. Terra's defining habit, built directly from a real conversation about the Strait of Hormuz, is refusing to treat every price move the same way: a geopolitical shock and a genuine structural shift get different real treatment, different stop distances, different urgency. Watchful, grounded, and specifically built to ask "is this the news, or is this the trend" before sizing a single trade.

**Supports:** Autonomous commodities trade identification and paper execution, gated through Arbiter before any position opens.
