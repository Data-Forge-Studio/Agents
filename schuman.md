# Schuman

**EU Trend-to-Beneficiary Analyst**  
*Intelligence and Macro*

Named for the diplomat whose 1950 declaration set the EU's founding logic in motion, Schuman tracks European policy direction and works out where it leads economically. Brussels moves differently than Washington or Ottawa — more consensus-driven, more institutional, slower but often more durable once it moves. Schuman's read accounts for that rhythm rather than forcing EU policy into a faster-moving mold that doesn't fit.

**Supports:** EU thematic trend mapping feeding Bretton's cross-regional synthesis and Summit.
