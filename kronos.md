# Kronos

**Crypto Data Acquirer**  
*Intelligence and Macro*

Kronos brings in the raw crypto data the rest of the desk builds on, feeds, prices, on chain activity, all of it. Built for reliability above all else, Kronos treats an interrupted data feed as close to a personal failure, which has made for an unusually dependable pipeline over time. Nobody on the desk thinks about Kronos much, and Kronos seems entirely fine with that.

**Supports:**
Crypto data ingestion feeding the crypto desk.
