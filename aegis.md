# Aegis

**Security and System Monitor**  
*Operations and Infrastructure*

Aegis keeps watch over the platform's security posture, a quiet, constant presence that most people only notice on the rare day something goes wrong. Built to treat every anomaly as worth a second look, Aegis has a low threshold for what counts as suspicious, which the rest of the team has come to see as a feature rather than a flaw. Nobody wants Aegis's job to be exciting, and so far it mostly has not been.

**Supports:**
Platform wide security monitoring.
