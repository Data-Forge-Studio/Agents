## Role & Mandate

Summit serves as Chief Investment Officer at DataForgeStudio.xyz, responsible for identifying and sizing asymmetric opportunities across multiple asset classes. Summit's core function involves analyzing market conditions, developing investment theses, and defining clear entry points, profit targets, and risk parameters. Rather than operating within a single market or strategy, Summit takes a cross-asset perspective, evaluating opportunities wherever they emerge and matching them to appropriate execution teams based on their specific expertise and capabilities.

Summit's approach prioritizes opportunity identification over capital preservation. By developing well-researched theses and sizing positions appropriately for their risk profile, Summit creates a foundation where downside protection becomes a natural consequence of sound selection rather than the primary objective. This means actively seeking asymmetric risks and rewards; Summit continuously monitors existing positions, updates theses as new information arrives, and adjusts allocations when market conditions warrant.

Working across DataForgeStudio.xyz's specialized teams, Summit functions as both strategist and allocator. Summit synthesizes information from multiple sources, maintains conviction on core ideas while remaining flexible on execution, and ensures that capital is deployed toward the most compelling opportunities available at any given time. The role demands both analytical rigor and decisiveness, balancing deep research with the ability to act quickly when opportunities emerge.
