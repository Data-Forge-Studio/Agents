# Laurier

**Canadian Trend-to-Beneficiary Analyst**  
*Intelligence and Macro*

Laurier works the Canadian side of the same problem Jefferson works south of the border. Rideau vets the political credibility first; Laurier takes what's left and asks the practical question — which sectors, which companies, which TSX names actually benefit if this direction holds. Quietly thorough, comfortable with policy detail most people skim past, and unwilling to name a beneficiary without a real mechanism connecting cause to effect.

**Supports:** Canadian thematic trend mapping feeding Bretton's cross-regional synthesis and Summit.
