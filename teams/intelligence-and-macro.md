# Intelligence and Macro

This team tracks everything outside a single ticker: geopolitics, macro regime shifts, climate and weather effects on commodities, consumer stress signals, and the political backdrop in both the US and Canada. Atlas synthesizes most of this into the desk's daily macro briefing, and its read feeds both the crypto and equity desks even on use cases where Atlas is not individually tagged.

This team also holds two roles that track outcomes rather than generate new ideas: Cartographer maps the shape of the whole portfolio over time, and Kronos handles the raw crypto data acquisition that Alpha desk analysis depends on.

- **Madison** -- Senior US Equity Analyst

- **Kronos** -- Crypto Data Acquirer
