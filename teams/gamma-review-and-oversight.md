# Gamma: Review and Oversight

This group exists to check the desk's own work, before and after the fact. Contrarian argues against a signal before it ever reaches Discord. Auditor and Cartographer review afterward, from two different angles: Auditor watches system and pipeline health, Cartographer watches the shape of the portfolio itself. Counsel takes the longest view of the three, asking on a weekly cadence whether the whole approach is still working.

Auditor's review is about the system's health, not a per-trade signoff, which is why it is not listed on individual [Use-Cases](https://github.com/Data-Forge-Studio/Use-Cases) files the way Cartographer is.

## Members

- **Auditor** -- System Intelligence and Performance Auditor
- **Cartographer** -- Portfolio and Equity Curve Tracker
- **Counsel** -- Strategic Trading Advisor
