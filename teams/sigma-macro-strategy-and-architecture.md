# Sigma: Macro, Strategy, and Architecture

The largest group, and the hardest to describe in one sentence, because it holds three genuinely different jobs. Summit and Contrarian sit here as the two roles that touch nearly every trading decision the desk makes, regardless of which desk it came from. Atlas, Nixon, and Rideau cover macro and political context. Strategist and Axiom cover the desk's own strategy and technical history. Scribe and Tally handle requirements and cost tracking. Lens watches the platform's own health.

It is a wide net because these roles share one thing in common: none of them are tied to a single desk's day to day trades. They operate above or across the desks rather than inside one of them.

## Members

- **Summit** -- Chief Investment Officer
- **Contrarian** -- Devil's Advocate
- **Atlas** -- Geopolitical and Macro Intelligence Analyst
- **Nixon** -- US Analyst
- **Rideau** -- Canadian Analyst
- **Strategist** -- Portfolio Strategist and Playbook Advisor
- **Axiom** -- System Knowledge and Architecture Authority
- **Scribe** -- Business Requirements and Architecture Authority
- **Tally** -- Backlog and Billing Tracker
- **Lens** -- System Observability Agent
