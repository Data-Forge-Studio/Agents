# Signals and Market Analysis

This is the largest team on the desk, and it does the actual market reading: crypto analysis, equity analysis, technical charting, and the alerting and data plumbing that both desks depend on. Oracle leads crypto analysis, Athena leads equity analysis, and Volta's technical read rides alongside both.

This team also holds a few specialists with narrower scope: Specter watches the wildest corner of crypto (meme coins and new launches), Garrison closes out the US trading day with an end of day summary, and Prometheus keeps an eye on prediction markets as an outside check on the desk's own convictions.

- **Athena** -- Senior Stock Market Analyst, equity desk lead
- **Volta** -- Technical Analysis and Chart Reading Specialist (shared across both desks)

- **Axiom** -- System Knowledge and Architecture Authority

## A note on Garrison vs Madison

Garrison and Madison both cover the US market but do different jobs. Madison (Intelligence and Macro team) does forward looking equity research that feeds into a specific trade thesis before a position opens. Garrison summarizes what already happened during the trading day, after the fact, for every US-focused position at once. Garrison is not a per-use-case research contributor the way Madison is, which is why Garrison does not appear on individual use case rosters in the [Use-Cases](https://github.com/Data-Forge-Studio/Use-Cases) repo the same way Madison does.
