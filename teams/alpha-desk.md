# Alpha Desk

The crypto desk. Oracle leads market analysis here, Cipher and Kronos handle the data layer, Specter watches the wildest corner of the market (meme coins and new launches), Sentinel decides what is worth alerting on, and Volta's technical read rides alongside all of it.

This is also the one desk where paper execution is genuinely active today: Trader tests real crypto strategies against real prices, which is why Trader shows up on individual Alpha use cases in the [Use-Cases](https://github.com/Data-Forge-Studio/Use-Cases) repo even though it lives in its own Execution group here.

## Members

- **Oracle** -- Senior Crypto Market Analyst, desk lead
- **Cipher** -- Crypto Data Analyst
- **Kronos** -- Crypto Data Acquirer
- **Specter** -- Meme Coin and New Launch Monitor
- **Sentinel** -- Crypto Alert and Notification Manager
- **Volta** -- Technical Analysis and Chart Reading Specialist (also supports the Beta Desk)
