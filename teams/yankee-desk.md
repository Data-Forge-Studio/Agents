# Yankee Desk

A dedicated US-market desk, distinct from the general Beta Desk. Madison does the forward-looking research that feeds a trade thesis before a position opens. Hamilton digs into US company fundamentals with the same rigor Sterling applies elsewhere. Garrison closes out the trading day with an end of day summary for whatever is open on this desk.

Garrison and Madison do different jobs (after-the-fact reporting versus forward research), but they are the same desk, which is why Garrison appears alongside Madison on this desk's use cases in the [Use-Cases](https://github.com/Data-Forge-Studio/Use-Cases) repo, credited specifically for end of day context rather than research contribution.

## Members

- **Madison** -- Senior US Equity Analyst
- **Hamilton** -- Fundamental Financial Analyst, US Markets
- **Garrison** -- US Market End of Day Briefing
