# Office of the CIO

The Office of the CIO sets strategy, sizes risk, and holds final say across every desk. Where the Signals and Intelligence teams generate ideas, this team decides what actually happens with them: whether a thesis is strong enough to act on, whether a position size makes sense against the whole portfolio, and whether last month's playbook still holds up.

This team also includes the desk's built-in skepticism. Contrarian exists specifically to argue against a signal before it reaches anyone else, and Sentinel filters crypto noise so only genuinely time-sensitive moves get surfaced.

## Members

- **Summit** -- Chief Investment Officer, final sign-off on strategy and sizing
- **Sterling** -- Fundamental Financial Analyst
- **Contrarian** -- Devil's Advocate, pre-alert stress testing
- **Counsel** -- Strategic Trading Advisor, weekly review
- **Sentinel** -- Crypto Alert and Notification Manager

## How this team fits in

Summit and Contrarian are the two members whose work genuinely touches every trading signal the desk produces, regardless of which desk or use case it came from. See [ORG-CHART.md](../ORG-CHART.md) for how that compares to the rest of the org.
