# Delta: Operations and Infrastructure

This group keeps the platform itself running: security, backups, the build pipeline, and the public site. None of these roles touch trading decisions directly. If a use case's research or execution is working correctly, it's in part because this group is doing its job well enough that nobody has to think about it.
