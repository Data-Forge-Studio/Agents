# Communications and Data

This team handles the flow of information into and around the desk: turning a rough idea into a properly scoped requirement, pulling in commodity and equity data feeds, and keeping track of what things cost. Vanguard's commodity research is the one role here that also shows up directly on trading use cases, specifically the macro-sensitive ones alongside Atlas.

## Members

- **Scribe** -- Business Requirements and Architecture Authority
- **Vanguard** -- Commodities and Futures Analyst
- **Hermes** -- Stocks Data Acquirer
- **Tally** -- Backlog and Billing Tracker

## A note on Vanguard

Vanguard's department here is Communications and Data, but its commodity research feeds the same macro-sensitive use cases Atlas covers (sector rotation, energy income). See [ORG-CHART.md](../ORG-CHART.md) for how that cross-team relationship works.
