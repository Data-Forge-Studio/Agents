# Execution

A standalone swarm of one, and the desk's paper trading layer. Trader takes an approved thesis and tests it against real prices without risking real capital.

## Current scope

Trader's paper execution currently runs on the Alpha Desk (crypto). Equity execution is not yet active or scheduled. Trader is tagged on individual [Use-Cases](https://github.com/Data-Forge-Studio/Use-Cases) files only where paper execution genuinely applies today.
