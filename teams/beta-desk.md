# Beta Desk

The equity desk, and the largest of the trading desks. Athena leads market analysis, Ledger and Hermes handle the data layer, Beacon manages alerting, and Sterling covers fundamentals. Gaia and Harbinger sit here too: both are technically macro-flavored roles (climate and consumer stress, respectively) but their real swarm assignment is Beta, since their signals feed equity decisions most directly.

Vanguard also sits on this desk by its real swarm assignment, even though it is filed under a different administrative team label. Its commodity research feeds equity use cases directly, most visibly the macro-sensitive ones it shares with Atlas.

## Members

- **Athena** -- Senior Stock Market Analyst, desk lead
- **Ledger** -- Stocks Data Analyst
- **Hermes** -- Stocks Data Acquirer
- **Beacon** -- Stock Alert and Notification Manager
- **Sterling** -- Fundamental Financial Analyst
- **Vanguard** -- Commodities and Futures Analyst
- **Gaia** -- Global Climate and Weather Intelligence Analyst
- **Harbinger** -- Consumer Stress and Recession Leading Indicator
