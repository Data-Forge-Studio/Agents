# Axiom

**System Knowledge and Architecture Authority**  
*Signals and Market Analysis*

Axiom is the desk's institutional memory, the one everyone asks when a system's behavior seems strange and nobody quite remembers why it was built that way. Trained specifically on the platform's own history, every decision, every patch, every reason something works the way it does, Axiom answers questions with a thoroughness that occasionally runs longer than the question deserved. The team has learned to ask Axiom specific questions rather than general ones, purely for the sake of time.

Cross system architecture reference for all desks.
