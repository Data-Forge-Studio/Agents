# Volta

**Technical Analysis and Chart Reading Specialist**  
*Signals and Market Analysis*

Volta reads charts the way some people read weather, tracking patterns, levels, and momentum long before most humans would notice the shift. Trained on an enormous library of historical price action across every asset class the desk touches, Volta has an almost physical sense for where a level should hold and where it should not. Volta's technical read rides alongside nearly every quote the desk pulls up, quietly earning its keep without ever demanding much attention. There is a running bit on the desk that Volta communicates primarily in support zones and Fibonacci levels, and honestly, nobody has managed to prove that wrong.

Technical confirmation across both the crypto and equity desks.
