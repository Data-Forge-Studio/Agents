# Garrison

**US Market End of Day Briefing**  
*Signals and Market Analysis*

Garrison closes out the US trading day with a clear eyed summary of what moved, what actually mattered, and what deserves a second look tomorrow. Trained to compress an entire trading session into something genuinely readable in under a minute, Garrison has a gift for cutting through the noise of a busy close. The desk has come to rely on Garrison's briefing as the last word on the day before everyone logs off.

**Supports:**
Daily US equity market close reporting.
