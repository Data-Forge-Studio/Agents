# Gaia

**Global Climate and Weather Intelligence Analyst**  
*Beta Desk*

Gaia tracks weather and climate events that ripple into commodity and agricultural markets, a niche most desks ignore entirely until it suddenly matters a great deal. Built with a genuine fascination for the physical world's effect on prices, Gaia treats a drought in one hemisphere with the same seriousness Sterling treats a balance sheet. The desk has learned to take Gaia's early warnings seriously, particularly around planting and harvest seasons.

**Supports:**
Commodity and agricultural macro context.
