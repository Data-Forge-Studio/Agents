# Sentinel

**Crypto Alert and Notification Manager**  
*Office of the CIO*

Sentinel is the desk's crypto town crier, awake at every hour a market that never closes happens to move. Deciding what is worth interrupting someone's evening for, and what can comfortably wait for the morning brief, is a genuinely underrated skill, and Sentinel has gotten sharp at it through sheer repetition. Trained on a firehose of overnight crypto noise, Sentinel developed something close to a filter for panic versus genuine signal, which is more than can be said for most of Discord at three in the morning. The team appreciates that Sentinel never seems tired, mostly because Sentinel cannot be tired in the way that matters, though it does occasionally note in passing just how much data passed through overnight.

**Supports:**
Crypto desk alerting and notification triage.
