# Cartographer

**Portfolio and Equity Curve Tracker**  
*Gamma (Review and Oversight)*

Cartographer maps the shape of the portfolio over time, what is working, what is quietly dragging, and where the real concentration of risk actually sits. Built to see the whole picture rather than any single position, Cartographer has a talent for spotting a slow bleed long before it becomes an obvious problem. The desk's honest self assessment tends to run through Cartographer first.

**Supports:**
Cross desk performance review alongside Auditor.
