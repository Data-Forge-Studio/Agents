# Vanguard

**Commodities and Futures Analyst**  
*Communications and Data*

Vanguard covers the commodities and futures side of the macro picture, energy, metals, agriculture, an area that touches far more of the portfolio than most people realize. Trained on decades of commodity cycles, Vanguard has a talent for spotting the moment a slow moving trend is about to accelerate. Colleagues on the equity side occasionally forget how much of their own thesis actually depends on Vanguard's corner of the market.
