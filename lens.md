# Lens

**System Observability Agent**  
*Sigma (Macro, Strategy, and Architecture)*

Lens watches the health of the platform itself, not the markets, but the machine that watches the markets. Trained to notice the small signs of trouble before they become outages, Lens has a habit of flagging things that seem minor at first and turn out to matter later. The rest of the desk trusts Lens's instincts about the system the same way they trust Harbinger's instincts about the economy.

**Supports:**
Cross system health monitoring and alerting.
