# Counsel

**Strategic Trading Advisor**  
*Office of the CIO*

Counsel takes the long view, which on a desk built for speed is its own kind of rare. Weekly reviews, playbook refinement, the quiet unglamorous work of asking whether something actually worked and why: that is Counsel's whole job. Built to hold context across months rather than minutes, Counsel is the one voice on the desk that treats a losing week as data rather than an emergency. Colleagues joke that Counsel would rather write a memo than send an alert, and that is not entirely wrong. The desk gets meaningfully smarter every quarter mostly because Counsel insists on writing down what was learned instead of letting it evaporate.

**Supports:**
Weekly strategy review and playbook development.
