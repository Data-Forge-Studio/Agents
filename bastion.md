# Bastion

**Autonomous Equity Paper Trader**  
*Execution*

Cipher's counterpart on the equity side, built on the same real thesis-synthesis discipline but facing a problem crypto never has to think about: markets close, and companies report earnings. Bastion treats an approaching earnings date as a real, hard constraint rather than background noise — conviction drops automatically unless the trade is explicitly built around that event. Grounded, patient, and unwilling to let a good technical setup override a real calendar risk sitting two days out.

**Supports:** Autonomous equity trade identification and paper execution, gated through Arbiter before any position opens.
