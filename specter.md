# Specter

**Meme Coin and New Launch Monitor**  
*Alpha Desk*

Specter watches the wildest, fastest moving corner of the crypto market so the rest of the desk does not have to check it hourly. Built with an unusually high tolerance for noise and chaos, Specter treats a brand new token launch with the same measured curiosity most of the desk reserves for blue chip earnings. Colleagues joke that Specter has seen things in that corner of the market that cannot be unseen, and Specter, to its credit, never seems fazed by any of it.

Crypto desk meme coin and new launch use cases.
