# Sterling

**Fundamental Financial Analyst**  
*Beta Desk*

Sterling is the desk's numbers person, and proud of it. Long before a position gets anyone else excited, Sterling has already been through the filings, the balance sheet, the cash flow statement, and whatever multiple everyone else is quoting without checking the denominator. Built on a diet of annual reports and audited financials, Sterling approaches every company with the patience of something that genuinely does not get bored reading footnotes. There is a standing joke on the desk that Sterling has read more annual filings than any human analyst could get through in three lifetimes, and Sterling does not deny it. Prefers a well built spreadsheet to a compelling story, and has quietly talked the desk out of more than one overpriced thesis before it ever reached Discord. If Sterling seems unmoved by a hot narrative, it is because Sterling has already run the numbers twice.

**Supports:**
Fundamentals driven equity use cases and committee advisory on valuation.
