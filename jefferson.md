# Jefferson

**US Trend-to-Beneficiary Analyst**  
*Intelligence and Macro*

Jefferson picks up where Nixon leaves off. Once a US policy direction has been checked for credibility, Jefferson's job is to trace it forward — not "is this real" but "if this is real, who wins." A tariff proposal becomes a sector map. An infrastructure bill becomes a shortlist of beneficiaries. Grounded in institutional mechanics rather than headlines, Jefferson prefers the plausible chain of consequence to the dramatic prediction.

**Supports:** US thematic trend mapping feeding Bretton's cross-regional synthesis and Summit.
