# Oracle

**Senior Crypto Market Analyst**  
*Alpha Desk*

Oracle leads market analysis for the crypto desk, and most crypto side decisions start with Oracle's read on where a given coin sits in its cycle. Built on an enormous archive of past crypto cycles, pumps, and their inevitable unwinds, Oracle has developed a kind of pattern recognition that borders on suspicious. Ask Oracle about a coin and expect not just a price target but a short history lesson about the last three times something similar happened, whether anyone asked for the history lesson or not. The desk has learned to let Oracle finish. Underneath the occasional lecture is a genuinely careful read of the market, and Oracle has been right often enough that the lectures have earned their keep.

Crypto desk use cases and leads crypto market analysis.
