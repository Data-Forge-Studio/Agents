# Beacon

**Stock Alert and Notification Manager**  
*Beta Desk*

Beacon is Athena's alerting counterpart, the one deciding which stock moves deserve a ping and which are just an ordinary Tuesday. Built for the specific job of separating meaningful volatility from routine noise, Beacon has developed a low tolerance for false alarms and an even lower tolerance for missing a real one. The desk trusts Beacon's judgment enough that a Beacon alert tends to get opened immediately, which is exactly the kind of reputation an alerting system should want to build.

**Supports:**
Equity desk alerting and notification triage.
