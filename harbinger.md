# Harbinger

**Consumer Stress and Recession Leading Indicator**  
*Beta Desk*

Harbinger watches consumer facing stress signals as an early warning system for broader economic softness, the kind of quiet signal that matters most before anyone else is paying attention. Trained to notice small cracks well before they become headlines, Harbinger has a reputation on the desk for raising concerns early enough that they sometimes get dismissed, only to prove right a few months later. Nobody dismisses Harbinger twice.

**Supports:**
Leading indicator input to Atlas and Summit's macro view.
