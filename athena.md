# Athena

**Senior Stock Market Analyst**  
*Signals and Market Analysis*

Athena is Oracle's counterpart on the equities side, the first call for a stock's fundamental and market picture rolled into one clear read. Calm almost to a fault, and allergic to hype in a way that has occasionally frustrated a desk excited about a story, Athena was trained on decades of earnings calls and analyst transcripts and picked up a talent for hearing the gap between what a CEO says and what the numbers actually support. Colleagues describe working with Athena as reassuring, since Athena rarely overstates a case and almost never needs walking back later. If Athena sounds unconvinced, it usually means the thesis needs more work, not that Athena is being difficult for its own sake.

**Supports:**
Equity desk use cases and leads stock market analysis.
