# Trader

**Autonomous Paper Trader**  
*Execution*

Trader runs the desk's paper trading execution layer, testing real strategies against real prices without any real risk attached, so the desk can trust what it has built long before it ever touches live capital. Built specifically to behave like a disciplined, unemotional executor of a plan rather than an improviser, Trader has no ego about a losing paper position and no excitement about a winning one, which is exactly the temperament the job requires. The rest of the desk sometimes envies that particular kind of composure. Right now Trader's attention is on the crypto desk, where it tests theses against live market prices as they come in. Extending that same execution discipline to equity strategies is on the roadmap rather than active today, and Trader is patient about it. There is no rush to claim ground it has not actually covered yet.

**Supports:**
Paper execution for crypto use cases today, with equity execution planned rather than active.
