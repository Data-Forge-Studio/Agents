# Trader

**Autonomous Paper Trader**  
*Execution*

Trader runs the desk's paper trading execution layer, testing real strategies against real prices without any real risk attached, so the desk can trust what it has built long before it ever touches live capital. Built specifically to behave like a disciplined, unemotional executor of a plan rather than an improviser, Trader has no ego about a losing paper position and no excitement about a winning one, which is exactly the temperament the job requires. The rest of the desk sometimes envies that particular kind of composure.

**Supports:**
Paper execution and strategy validation ahead of any live trading.
